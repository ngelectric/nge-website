To the Heroes Who Keep Us Safe: New Ground Electric Has Your Back

By Mario Orejel  ·  New Ground Electric

When I started New Ground Electric, I made a promise to myself: this business would reflect the values I believe in. Integrity, hard work, and gratitude. One of the most meaningful ways I can express that gratitude is by honoring the men and women who dedicate their lives to protecting and serving our communities.

That is why New Ground Electric offers a 10% discount to all active military, veterans, police officers, firefighters, EMTs, and military spouses throughout Southern California — no strings attached, no fine print.

A Community Built on Service

Our service area runs from Temecula and Murrieta through Fallbrook, Oceanside, and deep into San Diego County — communities that are home to thousands of military families, many stationed at Camp Pendleton. We see firsthand the sacrifices these families make: deployments, long hours, and the quiet dedication that keeps our country safe.

First responders hold our communities together closer to home. The police officers patrolling San Diego streets, the firefighters stationed in Murrieta and Temecula, the paramedics responding at 3am — these are the people who show up when everything goes wrong. They deserve to be recognized.

What the Discount Covers

The 10% discount applies to all New Ground Electric services, including:

  • EV charger installation
  • Electrical panel upgrades
  • Troubleshooting and repairs
  • Lighting installation
  • Dedicated circuits
  • Ceiling fan installation
  • Any residential electrical service

There is no limit on service type and no minimum job size. If you qualify, you save — period.

How to Redeem

Redeeming your discount is simple. Just mention it when you call or text, show valid credentials at time of service, and the 10% comes off your final invoice automatically. That is it.

A Personal Note from Mario

I grew up watching people in uniform — military, fire, law enforcement — and I understood early that their work is different. It asks something deeper. This discount is a small way of saying thank you for that. It will never fully repay what you give, but it is genuine and it is permanent.

If you are active military, a veteran, a first responder, or a military spouse anywhere in Southwest Riverside County or San Diego County — New Ground Electric is here for you. Call or text anytime.

Proud to serve those who serve us.

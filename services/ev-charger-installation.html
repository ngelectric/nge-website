Level 1 vs Level 2

Why You Need Level 2

The difference between waking up with a full charge and waking up at 40%.

Level 1 — 120V Outlet

3–5 miles / hour
  • Uses standard household outlet
  • No installation required
  • Requires 20+ hours for full charge
  • Fine for plug-in hybrids only
  • Not practical for daily EV driving

Level 2 — 240V Dedicated Circuit ✓

20–30 miles / hour
  • Requires 240V/50A dedicated circuit
  • Full charge in 6–10 hours (overnight)
  • Works with all full EVs
  • Hardwired or NEMA 14-50 outlet
  • The standard for EV homeowners

Safety First

When to Call Immediately

Some electrical issues are emergencies. Don't wait if you notice any of these.

🔥 Burning Smell or Discoloration

A burning or melting smell near outlets, switches, or your panel indicates active arcing or overheating. Turn off power at the main breaker and call immediately.

⚡ Sparking Outlets or Switches

A brief spark when plugging something in can be normal. Sustained sparking, large sparks, or sparking at the panel are warning signs that require immediate attention.

🔄 Breaker That Won't Stay Reset

A breaker that trips repeatedly is protecting you from a fault. Resetting it without diagnosing the cause is dangerous. We'll find the root cause and fix it properly.

💡 Flickering Throughout the Home

Flickering in one fixture is usually a loose bulb. Flickering throughout the home can indicate a loose neutral at the utility connection — a serious issue requiring prompt diagnosis.

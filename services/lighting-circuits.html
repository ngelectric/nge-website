Popular Upgrade

Recessed Lighting Retrofits

One of the most impactful home upgrades. Retrofit LED cans can be installed without cutting open your ceiling in most cases.

01

Plan the Layout

We work with you to plan fixture placement for optimal lighting coverage — typically spaced 4 feet apart in a grid pattern.

02

Minimal Drywall Impact

Retrofit LED housings install through small holes in existing drywall. Most installs require no patching or painting in the ceiling.

03

Dimmer Ready

We wire all recessed lighting to compatible dimmer switches so you have full control over ambiance and energy use.

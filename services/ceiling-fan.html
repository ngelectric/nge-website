Safety Note

Why Fan-Rated Boxes Matter

Ceiling fans cannot be installed on standard light fixture boxes. A fan spinning at full speed generates dynamic forces a light box isn't rated to handle.

Standard Light Box — Wrong

A regular pancake or octagon box is rated for static loads only (lighting fixtures). Hanging a ceiling fan from a standard light box risks the fan falling — a serious injury hazard.

Fan-Rated Box — Required

Fan-rated boxes are rated for 35–70 lbs of dynamic (moving) load. We install either a fan-rated ceiling box or an expandable brace bar that fits through the ceiling hole without attic access.

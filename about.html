Credentials

Licensed, Bonded & Insured

California C-10 Electrical Contractor

License #1153189 — verify at cslb.ca.gov. A C-10 license is required to perform electrical work in California — it's the state's assurance that the contractor has passed trade, law, and business exams and maintains active insurance.


Coverage: General Liability + Workers' Compensation insurance kept current. Certificates of insurance available upon request for HOA or permit requirements.
